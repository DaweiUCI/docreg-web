Help FAQ

I can't login...

Please use your GNET account username and password, e.g. "sabernethy". If this fails, it may be due to temporary lock-out of your GNET account - please wait 20 minutes and try again, or contact your local IT department. If failure persists please send an email to docreg@aviatnet.com.

Not authorized?

You may not be authorized to use the new secure DocReg service. If you think this is in error, or if you would like to be granted access for the first time, please email docreg@aviatnet.com or speak with Nancy Duran.

My downloads are very slow...

Are you using the DocReg server closest to your location? Check the list of servers in the sidebar on the right-hand side of this page.

Can I edit documents with this service?

Yes. You can try editing and submitting a test document to familiarise yourself with this feature.

  • Document Register Test - SC
  • Document Register Test - NZ

Can I create new documents with this service?

Yes. Use the create document feature on the menu bar.

I wish this service would do X, or would not do Y...

Great! We welcome your feedback. Please add your feature ideas, defect reports, or general comments to the feedback wiki page or email docreg@aviatnet.com.

What browser should I be using?

For best results we recommend you use a recent version of Google Chrome or Mozila Firefox. Use of Internet Explorer may result in problems...
